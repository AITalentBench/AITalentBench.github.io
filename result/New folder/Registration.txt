To register for the ICAIF 2023 Conference, each team must complete ICAIF-23 registration.

To register for this challenge, please refer to Participate section. Participants can also join the competition as a team of up to five members; for details of setting up as a team, please refer to User_Teams. Please ensure that all team members provide their full names, emails, and team names. We encourage you to use your institution email to register.
